## Folded into existing operators

### `fluidity-of-friends-foils-foes` (Ch. 5)
The observation that Trump's relationships rotate seamlessly through categories (friend → foe →
friend, etc.). Folded into operators 2 (rehabilitate) and 4 (drown-squeaky-wheel-in-oil)
because fluidity is the pattern those operators produce, not a distinct operator itself.

### `need-for-rotating-foils`
Ch. 5 observation that Trump requires a constant stream of adversaries. Belongs in skill #6
(`staying-focused-when-they-flood-the-zone`) — the mechanism is attention warfare, not
winner-sort calculus. Though a foil's role is to be attacked, the *selection* of which foil
comes from operator 5 here (selective retribution).

### `redeem-martyr-allies`
Stone/Manafort/Flynn/Bannon rehabilitation despite disreputable records. Folded into operator 2
as a variant: their "constituency" is their symbolic value in the leader's persecution
narrative, not a conventional audience. The operator captures this via its flexible definition
of constituency.

### `curated-companionship-symbolic` (WWE/UFC/athletes)
Ch. 7 material on Trump surrounding himself with Hulk Hogan, Dana White, Kid Rock, Tiger Woods,
Rory McIlroy. Belongs in skill #8 (grandeur) as the symbolic-value curation mechanism. Here it
would duplicate.
